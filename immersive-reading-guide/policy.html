Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01

Data Collection and Usage

Immersive Reading Guide is committed to protecting your privacy. Here's how we handle your data:

  • We only process webpage content to generate reading guides and highlights
  • All processing is done through your configured AI service
  • Your settings and preferences are stored locally in your browser
  • We do not collect any personal identification information
  • We do not track your browsing history or behavior

Required Permissions

Our extension requires certain permissions to function:

  • Storage: To save your preferences locally
  • Active Tab: To read and modify current webpage content for generating reading guides
  • Host Permissions: To function on webpages you choose to use it on

Data Sharing

We maintain strict data privacy:

  • We do not sell or share your data with any third parties
  • Webpage content is only processed through your configured AI service
  • All communication with AI services is done using your provided API key

Data Storage

All user data is stored locally:

  • Configuration settings are saved in your browser's local storage
  • No data is stored on our servers
  • You can clear all stored data by removing the extension

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • You can view and modify all settings at any time
  • You can disable the extension temporarily or remove it completely
  • Removing the extension will delete all locally stored data
